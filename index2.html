Welcome

Patients, carers, nurses and doctors in Scotland have come together to give you hope in the battle against pancreatic cancer.

This website is for sharing hope, knowledge and action.


Our Mission Statement

We're here to make a change. As we've said, we're here to share hope, knowledge and action. We want to make people more comfortable whether they be the patient, the carer or the relative. Pancreatic Cancer doesn't get as much as attention as what we would call 'The Big 4' and we want to change that but we can only do that with your help and your donations.
